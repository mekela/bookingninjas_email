Evolution of needs

Over a period of time, the basic needs known to us have changed. At the very first stages it was Food, Shelter and clothing. However, nowadays it has become so much more and one such important need is money. Along with money comes the need of time. Which means revolution has not only changed us, but also changed theories which were accepted by masses in the past.

For a company, Finance has been always the most important objective and therefore they have invested in strategy and marketing tactics to gain profits and increase sales while reducing overheads at the same time. However, similar to the evolution of needs of individual human beings, A company’s main need is not money anymore. It is Data that most companies thrive to collect.

As, if you have the proper data, you can stand out among the crowd easily with the insights and gaps assessed by the collected data.

Therefore, more than money being the motivator for Companies, Data has become the most important factor for various different reasons.
